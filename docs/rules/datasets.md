# Annex A: Essential Tasks – Suggested Problem Statements and Datasets

The AI Hackathon Theme for 2026 is Human Intelligence Driving Transformative Innovation

The list below aligns with Ireland’s National AI Strategy across the following domains: Agriculture, Healthcare, Housing, Justice & Public Safety, Economy & Finance, and Environment. Participants are encouraged to leverage datasets from sources such as data.gov.ie, CSO, Teagasc, and the European Data Portal

|Domain|Dataset Sources|Potential Topics|
|---|---|---|
|Agriculture|data.gov.ie, CSO, EPA, SEAI,<br>Teagasc|Crop disease management, pest control, soil health monitoring, weather forecasting,<br>irrigation optimisation, supply chain management, market analysis, regulatory compliance|
|Health|HSE, CSO, World Bank Open Data|Disease prevention, patient management, telemedicine, health data analysis, mental health<br>support, drug development, healthcare administration, patient education|
|Housing|housingagency.ie, CSO|Affordable housing, construction efficiency, urban planning, energy efficiency in housing,<br>smart housing solutions, homelessness prevention, community engagement|
|Population and<br>Society|data.gov.ie, CSO, Irish Legal<br>Information Initiative (IRLII)|Education enhancement, employment support, public safety, social services optimisation,<br>cultural preservation, policy analysis, disaster preparedness|
|Science and<br>Technology|European Data Portal,<br>data.europa.eu, World Bank Open<br>Data|R&D support, innovation acceleration, STEM education, data analysis, collaboration and<br>networking, public engagement in science, ethical considerations in technology|
|Transport|Transport Infrastructure Ireland (TII),<br>CSO, Eurostat|Traffic management, public transport optimisation, road safety improvement, autonomous<br>vehicle integration, sustainable transport solutions, emergency response coordination|
|Justice and<br>Safety|Irish Legal Information Initiative<br>(IRLII),CSO|Crime prevention, legal document automation, access to legal services, cybersecurity<br>enhancement, public safety awareness, fraud detection, judicial decision support|
